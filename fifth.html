Kids Engaging Tips.

Engaging kids while reading is key to fostering a love for books and learning. Here are some tips to make reading time fun and interactive for children:
1. Use expressive voices for different characters to make the story come alive.
2. Encourage kids to ask questions about the plot, characters, or illustrations.
3. Incorporate interactive elements like props or acting out scenes from the book.
4. Pause to discuss the story, ask for predictions, or relate it to their own experiences.
5. Make reading a bonding experience by cuddling up together and sharing the joy of storytelling.
By making reading engaging and interactive, you can help children develop a lifelong love for books and learning.
