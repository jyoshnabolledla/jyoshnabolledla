Toodlers Engaging Tips

Engaging toddlers while reading books for toddlers is key to making it fun and educational for them. Here are some tips to keep them engaged:
1. Use expressive voices for different characters.
2. Encourage them to point at things in the pictures.
3. Ask questions about the story to spark their imagination.
4. Let them interact with the book, like turning the pages or touching different textures.
5. Create a cozy reading environment to make it a special time together.
These techniques can help make reading time with toddlers exciting and interactive.
